Return & Refund Policy

At SmartStore, we value our customers and strive to provide a seamless shopping experience. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

7-Day Return Window

You have 7 working days from the date of delivery to initiate a return request for most items. Items must be returned in their original condition, unused, and with all original tags and packaging intact.

Eligible Items for Return

  • Products with manufacturing defects.
  • Products damaged during transit.
  • Wrong item delivered (incorrect size, color, or model).
  • Items that do not match the description on our website.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, certain categories are non-returnable:

  • Personal care products and innerwear.
  • Perishable goods like food and flowers.
  • Customized or personalized products.
  • Digital products (e-books, software keys).

Note: A small processing fee may apply for returns initiated due to "Change of Mind".

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it and notify you of the status. If approved, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method (Credit Card/UPI) or into your SmartStore wallet.

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, refunds are typically processed via bank transfer or store credit.

Need Help?

If you have any questions regarding our return policy, please feel free to reach out to our support team at support@smartecommerce.com or visit our Contact Us page.
